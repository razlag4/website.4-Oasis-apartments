At The Oasis Apartments, pets are considered part of the family. We are committed to providing a safe, comfortable, and responsible environment for all residents and their animal companions. This document outlines the comprehensive policies and regulations regarding pet ownership within our community.

Pet Registration & Approval

All pets must be officially registered with the management office prior to move-in. Registration requires:

  • Completed Pet Registration Form with owner contact information.
  • Recent vaccination records, including rabies, distemper, and other required vaccines.
  • Photo of each pet for identification purposes.
  • Proof of spay/neuter or a signed waiver if applicable.

Approval is mandatory before a pet is brought onto the property. Unregistered pets may result in fines or lease violations.

Allowed Species & Breed Restrictions

We welcome domestic dogs and cats with the following limitations:

  • Maximum of two pets per apartment unit.
  • Dogs may not exceed 60 lbs in weight.
  • Cats must be indoor-only and declawed is not required but highly recommended for safety.
  • Certain breeds of dogs considered aggressive or dangerous are prohibited. A full list is available upon request.
  • No exotic animals, reptiles, farm animals, or venomous species are permitted.

Service animals and emotional support animals are exempt from breed and weight restrictions, provided proper documentation is submitted.

Fees, Deposits & Financial Responsibilities

  • One-time pet deposit: $300 (non-refundable)
  • Monthly pet rent: $35 per pet
  • Additional cleaning or repair fees may apply if pets cause damage to the unit or common areas.
  • Residents are financially responsible for any damages caused by their pets, including walls, floors, furniture, and landscaping.
  • Insurance coverage for pet-related damages is recommended.

Behavior & Conduct Requirements

Residents must ensure their pets behave appropriately at all times. Unruly or aggressive pets can result in penalties or eviction. The following rules apply:

  • Pets must not disturb neighbors through excessive barking or noise.
  • Leashes are required in all common areas, including hallways, lobbies, and outdoor spaces.
  • Owners are required to immediately clean up after their pets.
  • Pets should not be left unattended for prolonged periods in the apartment.

Repeated violations may result in written warnings, fines, or termination of the lease agreement.

Pet-Friendly Amenities

On-site Dog Park with agility equipment
Pet Wash Station with hot/cold water
Access to nearby walking trails
Indoor Pet Lounge Area for socialization
Designated pet feeding stations in public spaces
Partnerships with local veterinarians

Safety & Health Guidelines

To maintain a safe community for all residents:

  • All pets must be current on vaccinations and health screenings.
  • Sick or aggressive animals must be removed from common areas until cleared by a veterinarian.
  • Residents are encouraged to have pets microchipped for identification.
  • Emergency protocols are in place in case of natural disasters or health incidents.

Inspection & Compliance

Management reserves the right to conduct periodic inspections to ensure compliance with pet policies. Inspections may include:

  • Verification of pet registration and vaccination records.
  • Observation of pet behavior and living conditions.
  • Checking for potential damage to units or common areas.

Residents will be notified in advance of inspections unless in case of emergency.

Owner Responsibilities & Liability

Pet owners are fully responsible for:

  • Providing proper nutrition, care, and veterinary attention.
  • Ensuring pets do not pose a threat to other residents or pets.
  • Covering costs of any damages or injuries caused by their pets.
  • Adhering to all municipal regulations and licensing requirements.

Insurance coverage for pet-related incidents is strongly recommended.
